Objectives of NAIMS

NAIMS strives to provide farmers, policymakers, and researchers with easy access to critical agricultural data and insights. This access enables informed decision-making, leading to improved agricultural practices and policies. NAIMS aims to centralize agricultural data from various sources, including farmers, research institutions, and government agencies, to create a comprehensive database of agricultural information...

Goalof NAIMS

NAIMS contributes to the sustainability of Nigeria's agricultural landscape. By promoting efficient resource utilization, reducing wastage, and optimizing production, it supports long-term sustainability goals. These objectives collectively aim to modernize and improve the Nigerian agricultural sector, leading to increased food security, economic growth, and overall development.
