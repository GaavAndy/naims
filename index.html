Goals of NAIMS

NAIMS contributes to the sustainability of Nigeria's agricultural landscape. By promoting efficient resource utilization, reducing wastage, and optimizing production, it supports long-term sustainability goals. These objectives collectively aim to modernize and improve the Nigerian agricultural sector, leading to increased food security, economic growth, and overall development.

Aim of NAIMS

NAIMS aims to centralize agricultural data from various sources, including farms, research institutions, and government agencies. This centralized data repository facilitates efficient data management and analysis. The system's goal is to make agricultural information readily accessible to farmers, researchers, policymakers, and other stakeholders.
